A mobile app sounds appealing, but it is an investment and a long-term commitment. Before you start, you need to ask whether it answers a real need or whether a good mobile website would be enough.

## When an app makes sense

An app makes the most sense when your customers come back often: loyalty programs, recurring orders, regular bookings. Push notifications let you stay in touch and remind people of your offers at the right moment — something a website does not do as well.

## When a good mobile site is enough

If your main goal is to be found and to present your business, a fast, well-ranked mobile site is often the better fit. It requires no download, works on every device, and stays simpler to maintain.
